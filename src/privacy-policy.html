Privacy Policy

This policy was last updated on 24 February 2023.

This privacy policy explains how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

What data do we collect?

We do not collect any personal information unless you opt-in via the following functionality provided by third-parties:

  • Our News page and News widgets are powered by AnnounceKit. You may opt-in to receive emails or Slack messages with Livebook-related news. Read their Privacy Policy.
  • You may choose to use Google Forms for answering surveys and reaching out to us, which may include your email address. Your email address will only be used for follow-ups related to your inquiry. Read their Terms.

Additional third-party integrations

When using our website, you will indirectly use the following third-party services:

  • We use Plausible Analytics for cookie-free privacy-friendly analytics. Read their Privacy Policy.
  • We use JSDELIVR (Privacy Policy) for serving fonts. Read their Privacy Policy.

The Livebook application

The Livebook Desktop application runs fully within your machine and does not transmit any personal information to our servers.

We use https://livebook.space/ to serve and safely run iframes within the Livebook application. The referenced website does not store any information.

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

Our website uses functional cookies to store your previously selected preferences and interactions. These could include which news you have already read and commented on. Third-party services linked to or embedded in our website may use their own cookies.

For any questions and concerns, email us at: privacy@livebook.dev
